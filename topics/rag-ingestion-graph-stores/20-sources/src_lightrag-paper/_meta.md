# Meta

LightRAG paper. Accepted at EMNLP 2025; v3 dated 2025-04-28. Authors from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications and University of Hong Kong. Positions itself as a lighter graph-RAG alternative to Microsoft GraphRAG: dual-level retrieval (low + high), graph + vectors combined, incremental update algorithm.
